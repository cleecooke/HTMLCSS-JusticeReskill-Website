First Days Out

Your first days out after incarceration can be overwhelming. Remerg.com will help you find what you need.

Your Restart List

  • Contact your parole or probation officer
  • Connect with a re-entry organization
  • Locate food, clothing, & other essentials
  • Obtain identification documents
  • Find housing
  • Take care of your physical and mental health
  • Know who to call if you have a crisis
  • Sign up for benefits at a human services office
  • Find a job or get help finding a job
  • Connect to people & places that support you
  • Register to vote!
